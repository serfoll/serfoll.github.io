Privacy Policy for Dogy App

Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how Jules Design Studio (organization number: 950508-5531), the owner of the Dogy app, collects, uses, and protects personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Personal Data Collected: Dogy app collects personal data such as names, contact details, and dog-related information (e.g., dog’s name, breed, and preferences) to provide and improve our service.

Usage Data: Information on how the service is accessed and used may also be collected.

Personal data is processed based on the user’s consent, the necessity for the performance of a contract, and the legitimate interests of Jules Design Studio.

User Rights

Under GDPR, users have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of their data, object to processing, and data portability.

Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including safeguarding against unauthorized or unlawful processing and against accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

Data Sharing

Personal data may be shared with third-party service providers who assist us in providing the service, always under strict data processing agreements.

International Data Transfers

Data may be transferred to countries outside the EU/EEA and will be protected by appropriate safeguards.

Changes to the Policy

Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be communicated through the Dogy app or website.

Contact Information

For any privacy-related inquiries, users can contact us at jules@dogy.app.

Effective Date
